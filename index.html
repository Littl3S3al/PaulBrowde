HEALING THROUGH CONNECTION

MY PHILOSPHY:

We are born in connection, we are wired for connection, and it is through connection that we heal and experience our true aliveness.

ABOUT ME:

For the past thirty years, I have worked as a clinician. As a doctor, therapist and couples’ guide, I have been trusted with people’s deeply personal experiences; their despair, their joy and their dreams.

I have performed my own personal life story for diverse audiences across the globe, guiding others how to listen, and to tell their own stories.

MY OFFERINGS:

Individual Therapy


My aim as a therapist is to listen deeply - and to have an authentic, connected relationship with - the particular individual who consults me.

INDIVIDUAL THERAPY

Relationship Guide


Being in a couple (or any relationship) is not a problem to be solved, but rather an opportunity for personal growth and intimacy through connection.

I teach couples how to connect in this way.

COUPLES THERAPY

Storytelling and Speaking


Stories are a way of making sense of life, building self-esteem, and creating intimacy and connection.

As a public speaker I use my life stories and professional expertise to facilitate experiences of learning and connection.
