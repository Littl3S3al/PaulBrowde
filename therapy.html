Talk Therapy

for couples and individuals

RELATIONSHIP GUIDANCE:

I work with a variety of relationship constellations, including couples, parents and children, and siblings. In all these relationships, I introduce people to specific techniques for accessing and decluttering the deep connected space that exists between them. Practicing these techniques under my guidance rewires the brain, laying down new neural pathways.

This Encounter-Centered approach takes place in long sessions, either over two consecutive days, or is broken down into shorter blocks spread out over weeks.

WHAT TO EXPECT AND HOW TO BOOK

INDIVIDUAL THERAPY:

I draw from a multitude of techniques and approaches. As a psychotherapist I may use insight oriented work, narrative therapy, mindfulness, and coaching. I am schooled in psychopharmacology, and if indicated I prescribe medications.

I offer individual psychiatric consultation and psychotherapy both in person in my office in Manhattan, or if appropriate, via video conferencing.
